LungCancer Detecttion

Deep learning for lung cancer detection involves training advanced algorithms, like Convolutional Neural Networks, on medical images to automatically recognize patterns associated with lung cancer. These models enable tasks such as segmentation and early detection, reducing false positives and negatives. Integration with clinical data enhances accuracy and allows for personalized medicine. While challenges exist, deep learning holds promise for revolutionizing lung cancer detection and improving patient outcomes.
